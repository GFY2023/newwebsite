ShyLynn Ranch is located in the beautiful Salmon River Valley. We are approximately 25 minutes from Salmon Arm, Vernon and Armstrong (1 hour from Kelowna and Kamloops).

If you need more help with the directions please don't hesitate to contact us.

Directions from Vernon

  1. Go North on Hwy 97 from Vernon
  2. Once North of Vernon take the exit for Hwy 97 North to Kamloops (Swan Lake Nursery is on the left)
  3. Go approx 18km and turn right onto Salmon River Road.
  4. Go 2km and turn left up the hill onto Yankee Flats Road (Log building on right and a set of mailboxes on left)
  5. Go approx 6km and you will see our sign on the right.
  6. Campground is 2km down the driveway. Please drive slow and watch for horseback riders on the driveway.
  7. Welcome to the Ranch!

Directions from Salmon Arm

  1. Leave Salmon Arm following Hwy 1 towards Chase/Kamloops
  2. Take exit for Silver Creek
  3. Follow first roundabout to go under Hwy 1
  4. Exit second roundabout for 10 Ave SW/Silver Creek
  5. Stay on main road through 5 90 degree turns
  6. 2 km past the Silver Creek General Store turn right onto Yankee Flats Road
  7. Go 7 km and you will see the Ranch sign on you left.
  8. Campground is 2km down the driveway. Please drive slow and watch for horseback riders on the driveway.
  9. Welcome to the Ranch!

Directions from Kamloops

  1. Take Hwy 1 East towards Salmon Arm/Vernon
  2. Take the exit for Hwy 97 South to Vernon
  3. After about 62km turn left on Salmon River Road towards Salmon Arm
  4. Go 2km and turn left up the hill onto Yankee Flats Road (Log building on right and a set of mailboxes on left)
  5. Go approx 6km and you will see our sign on the right.
  6. Campground is 2km down the driveway. Please drive slow and watch for horseback riders on the driveway.
  7. Welcome to the Ranch!
