Wedding Planning Services and Assists

  • Wedding Planning
    • We are here to guide and support you during your entire wedding planning process, we love it! We can book a time to sit down with you (as many appointments as you’d like) or of course we can do this over the phone and email as well, which is the most common since most of our couples are from out of town or out of country, lol. Weddings are truly what we do 24/7 and we can help you in ways you may have never even considered. Everything from catering ideas, bar menu, DJ’s, accommodation ideas both on and off site, decorations, your wedding day timeline (from getting ready in the morning to setting up a great photography schedule throughout your day), late night buffets, I could go on and on. The fun ‘extras’ too like lawn games that guests love to play, even planned activities like an Axe Throwing event, oh ya, we can help with some pretty fun ideas, lol. Keep in mind, this is a company you hire, our insurance doesn’t exactly let you just start tossing axes around the ranch, oh my! Lol. We can offer other ideas too like amazing money saving tips and so much more! Never be shy to keep an open line of communication with us because the more questions we get from you, the more we get to know your wedding, and we love it! I always tell our couples, the larger your wedding ‘file’ is, the more we know about what you want, and that’s a really great thing!
  • 'Day of' Wedding Coordination!
    • We’ll be with you and your guests during your entire day and throughout the evening to ensure everything goes as perfect as it should. There are so many details included in this service that I honestly couldn’t list them if I tried, lol. But I can promise you that we will take care of things in such a way that your only job will be to have the most amazing day of your life, and we’ll take care of the rest.
  • Check-in Service
    • We do a full ‘check-in’ with our couples on Friday morning (10am in the barn, not TOO early, lol). This is where I get a chance to sit down with you and go over every detail of your wedding, fulling timeline schedule, all of your vendors, when/if they’re delivering, as well as any services we need to assist with or take care of for you. It’s one of my favorite things to do with you because you’ve probably had a million things running through your head for the last few months, and this is where I get the chance to take all of your stress away and let you know that we’ve got you.
  • Full setup service!
    • We start decorating for your wedding on Friday morning, about 10-10:30am in the events barn.
    • This is when we’ll set up a ‘mock table’ to make sure your decorating vision has come to life, and we’ll take it from there while you hang out in the campground with your guests, so fun! Then come back a little while later and ‘fine tune’ anything you’d like. You can add more décor, change things up, it’s really quite easy. You just say what you’d like and we’ll make it happen. If you’d like to stay with us during the entire decorating process, that’s fun too!
  • Full takedown service!
    • By the time you wake up on Sunday morning we’ll already have all of your items gathered on the tables in a lovely way so that all you need to do is collect whatever goes home with you. You don’t even have to touch a broom or cleaning cloth, we do it all! Mom’s really love this part because they’re usually the ones who think they have clean up duty, lol.
  • Cocktail hour/appies & late night snack buffets (*we purchase these directly from local catering companies)
    • These two snacks are so loved by guests that after years of doing this, we’ve decided to include them! Seeing the difference between weddings that have these snacks available for guests, versus ones that don’t, we can honestly tell you that this is worth it, lol. But it’s a lot of work to now bring on two new vendors and the coordination that goes with it, so we’d sometimes see many wedding struggled with this. Do they do this through their dinner caterer? (that gets extremely costly because now you’ll also have travel and staff fees added on for several more hours). Do they bring they bring them in themselves? Yes, sometimes they would, but that also came with challenges, where to store the food in the meantime? Who does the prep and serving? Should they pick it up last minute (as if they don’t have enough last minute things to do, lol), where will they store it when here? Are they able to ensure that refrigerated items are kept food-safe while transporting over long distances? The stress goes on and on. Seeing our couples go through all the work involved in pulling these two meals together was tough. So now we’re including it!
    • Evening snack (often called a midnight buffet) is actually served around 9:30pm. We’ll actually gauge how your guests are doing (*cough…how they’re handling their alcohol, lol), if they look like they’re all getting a little too tipsy by 8:30/9pm we’ll serve this early to help them get some food in their stomach. If you’re dinner was a later one, and guests aren’t drinking a lot, we’ll serve it about 9:30/10pm. But 9:30 is generally the perfect moment. While you’re cocktail hour has wonderful selections of lighter foods, your evening one will offer more carbs and salts, giving them a little help to continue enjoying the evening (and hopefully not feel the hangover as much the next day too, lol). We make these little buffets so delicious that the guests just think they’re getting spoiled! They have no idea we’re actually looking out for them in an entirely different way as well.
    • Please note, we are not caterers and as much as we’d love to, we can not do your dinner as well, lol. Our snack buffets are amazing! Gorgeous, plentiful, and absolutely some of the best parts that your guests will enjoy! However, we are limited in our allowances due to licensing. We adhere to amazing items that do not require cooking and certain food handling elements, we simply purchase them from preferred locations and vendors (usually 2 or 3 so we can pick the best from the best, lol) but we do not have a licensed kitchen, and aren’t caterers, so we must let the professionals handle your dinner, lol.

    • o Another great service is setting up your dessert table. We typically do full set up and display about ½ hour before your cake cutting, that way your photographer has a perfect chance to take some great shots before it gets touched. This also helps prevent ‘wondering hands’ as guests are always tempted to sneak a little something from your dessert display in the afternoon, lol. As soon as you’re cake cutting is complete, we open your full dessert bar for your guests. We’d also be happy to cut your wedding cake for your guests if you’d like, we display it beautifully on your desert table and everything.

Please let your dessert person/baker know that once they drop the cupcakes, wedding cake (or whatever you might be serving) off with us at the barn (usually Saturday morning) we’ll be happy to take it from there, they are not required to set up your items for you, in fact, it truly works better if they don’t (like we said…wondering hands, lol). We can even help by bringing these items in for you directly from our suppliers, never hesitate to ask!
  • Coffee and Tea services (supplies included)
    • This service will be offered to your guest right from your ceremony time and through the entire night. All of the cups and supplies will also be included so you have nothing to worry about.
  • Beverage service (supplies included)
    • This is typically iced tea & lemonade that we make for your guests as a lovely drink option, but if you’d like different flavors or options simply let us know ahead of time so we can ensure we have it for you.

    • Florists are amazing! And that usually means they’ve got many clients on the go the same weekend as you, lol. Please let them know they are absolutely welcome to simply drop the items off at the ranch, and we’d be happy to set up your arbor, ceremony site, head table, guest tables, absolutely everything! All you need to do is let us know what you’ve ordered and any instructions you’d like to give us as to where certain things go, and we’ve got it from there. Let us help save you those ‘set up’ fees! We can do this with you on Friday so you have time to ‘play’ with the placement of the arrangements if you’d like, as this allows you plenty of time to offer any changes you might desire. If your greenery or florals are sensitive, we’ll simply do a mock table with you on Friday and ensure you’re 100% over the moon in love with it, then Saturday morning we repeat your perfect vision on the rest of your tables with the fresh greenery. We can honestly work with whatever you love.

It’s quite common for venues to ask that dishware be washed by the caterer, so mentioning that we have our own dishes AND that they don’t have to wash/rinse them after dinner service may help lower your catering quote as most caterers include that automatically.

    • Once your caterer leaves for the evening, your guest tables will still have some stray plates and dinner items. We’re happy to clear tables for you throughout your night. We actually do this service for you throughout the entire afternoon & evening, this especially comes in handy as your guest tables become filled with drink glasses, solo cups, dessert dishes, etc. We keep your tables tidy and your guests happy.
    • *Please note that your caterer is responsible for clearing dinner plates after dinner service. Our table clearing service is for before & after dinner, and for those few stragglers who hold onto their dinner plates a little longer, lol. Caterers always include this as part of their service , but it has become something we do need to mention to our couples now because as more and more caterers get to know how helpful we are, we are noticing that they are tempted to pass the dinner clearing service onto us, which we typically don’t find out until the moment guests are eating, and we are not staffed for this.

Guest Services

  • An entire weekend dedicated to you and your guests!
    • Check in is anytime after 2pm on Thursday, and check out is 12pm on Sunday.
  • Accommodations
    • Weddings include all camping fees so none of your guests have to pay to camp, you can even include it on your wedding invitations that the bride and groom have taken care of everything and the guests are welcome to camp for free! (guests really love that feature)
    • Guests who might like to stay here on site for the weekend but don’t have an RV are welcome to check out the local RV rentals (listed on our camping page). We have tons of guests every weekend that will arrange to have an RV brought here for them, the company sets it up and the guests camp for the weekend, then the company comes back to pick it up. These companies typically have a 3 night minimum which is why your weekend is absolutely welcome to start as early as 2pm on Thursday! Check isn’t until 12 noon on Sunday.
    • *Note: While guest arrival is anytime after 2pm on Thursday, RV Rental companies are welcome to deliver their RV anytime Thursday morning. And while guest checkout is 3pm on Sunday, your RV Rental company is welcome to pick up as late on Sunday as they like. We know that they also deliver to provincial and private campgrounds, and they typically have strict times that the RV companies need to abide by, so we allow them to arrive earlier than guests, and pick up after the guests have departed, this way they have more flexibility with us which we are happy to offer them.

  • Parking Attendant
